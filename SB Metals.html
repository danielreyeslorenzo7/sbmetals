Materials We Purchase

We accept the following materials. All must be clean and free of contamination.

Aluminum

65 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No plastics, other metals, wood, stones, dirt, paint, or sealants.

  • • Window profiles
  • • Sheets and plates
  • • Vehicle rims

Motors

30 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No plastic housings, external cables, wood bases, excessive iron screws, or contaminated grease.

  • • Washing machine motors
  • • Industrial motors
  • • Compressors with copper

Solid Iron

7 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No plastics, wood, concrete, dirt, household trash, or lead-based paint.

  • • Beams and angles
  • • Thick pipes
  • • Heavy scrap

Battery

30 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No broken plastic cases, loose caps with spilled acid, connected cables, or adhered trash.

  • • Car batteries
  • • Truck batteries
  • • Industrial batteries

Stainless Steel

30 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No adhered carbon steel, plastics, rubber, aluminum, copper, or food/grease residues.

  • • Pipes and fittings
  • • Industrial plates
  • • Kitchen equipment

Reefa

100 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No mixed broken aluminum fins, plastic pipes, excessive brass valves, or foam insulation.

  • • AC condensers
  • • Copper/aluminum radiators
  • • Air conditioning units

Brass

130 JMD/lb

Must be clean and uncontaminated: No lead seals, chrome/nickel plating, welded iron parts, plastics, or attached rubber.

  • • Faucets and valves
  • • Electrical connectors
  • • Decorations and accessories
